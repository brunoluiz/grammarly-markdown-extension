Grammarly to Markdown

Convert your Grammarly texts to Markdown

Download for Chrome Download for Firefox

Because writing is what matters

Every day, more is written and published on the internet. Many of these texts are written in English, by non-native speakers. Grammarly does a really good job helping writers to create bold and clear texts.

Markdown is a click away

Many might want to export documents in Markdown, but there is no official way to export texts to Markdown. This is where this extension will help you.

While in the document page, a simple click on the extension button (located on the browser toolbar) will do its magic. It will parse and copy the text to your clipboard, ready to be published in any markdown medium.
